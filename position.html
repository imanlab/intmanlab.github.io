    Commonwealth Scholarship and Fellowship and other scholarship for Indians

    The Commonwealth Scholarship and Fellowship
    is an international programme under which member governments offer scholarships and fellowships to citizens of other Commonwealth countries. The programme was established at the first Commonwealth Education Conference in 1959, and over 26,000 individuals have benefitted from it so far.
